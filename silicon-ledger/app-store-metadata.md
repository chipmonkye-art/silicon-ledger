## Description (4000 chars)
Silicon Ledger is a precision accounting engine designed for individuals and small businesses who demand accuracy, privacy, and speed.

**Built for accuracy**
Every transaction is stored as integer cents — no floating-point rounding errors. Multi-currency support with automatic FX rate updates. Double-entry principles without the complexity.

**Staging gate workflow**
Transactions enter as staged drafts. Review, approve, or reject with notes. Full audit trail captures every change. Never lose track of who changed what and when.

**Credit control & cash flow**
Set soft/hard credit limits per account. Get 30/60/90-day cash flow projections with expected, worst, and best-case scenarios. Aging analysis for receivables and payables.

**Offline-first sync**
Work without internet. Changes sync automatically when connected, with CRDT-based conflict resolution. Your data, always available.

**Biometric security**
Secure the app with face ID, Touch ID, or WebAuthn. Server-side session revocation. AES-256 encryption at rest. TLS 1.3 in transit.

**Smart search**
Search transactions by natural language: "show me expenses over $500 last month" — the NLP engine parses intent automatically. Voice search supported.

**Import your data**
Drag-and-drop Excel or CSV import with column mapping. Auto-detects headers and data types. Transforms values into the staging gate for review.

**Privacy first**
Your financial data belongs to you. GDPR-compliant account purge. No ads. No third-party tracking. Row-level security ensures workspace isolation.
